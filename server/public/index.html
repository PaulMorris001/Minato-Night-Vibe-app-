Tickets & discovery

Buy tickets in CityVibe — and discover everything else, too.

For events on CityVibe, you buy your ticket right in the app with secure checkout — fast, simple, all in one place. We also bring in live events from trusted partners like Ticketmaster, so your feed shows everything happening around you; for those, we link you straight to the partner to finish the purchase on their official page.

1 Discover an event in CityVibe
→
2 Tap “Get Tickets”
→
3 Pay in-app, or finish on the partner's site

Tickets for CityVibe events are sold and processed securely in the app. External events appear right alongside them in your feed, and their “Get Tickets” action opens the partner's official event page to complete the purchase there.
